# Ad 6 — Full Property Walkthrough Carousel
## "Your Home in Costa Brava"

---

## Concept
A complete property walkthrough — every room, every feature, one slide at a time. Designed to feel like a virtual tour that builds desire from the first swipe to the last. Targets anyone discovering Villa Victoria for the first time.

**Hook:** "Your Home in Costa Brava"
**Format:** 13 slides (1 hook + 11 spaces + 1 CTA)
**Platform:** Instagram carousel
**CTA:** DM on Instagram for availability

---

## Design Specs (All Slides)
- **Size:** 1080 × 1080px
- **Font:** Montserrat, white (#FFFFFF), semi-bold
- **Headline:** 26–28px, bottom-center
- **Subtext:** 18px, below headline, lighter weight
- **Overlay:** Dark semi-transparent black, 60% opacity, rounded corners 12px
- **Tone:** Warm, Mediterranean — like a friend showing you around their home
