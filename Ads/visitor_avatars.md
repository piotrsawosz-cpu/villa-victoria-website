# Villa Victoria — Target Audiences & Booking Motivations

---

## Target Audiences

**Wealthy Families with Children**
Parents in their 30s–50s travelling with kids. They want space, safety, and a private pool — not a hotel. They care about comfort, outdoor space, and having activities nearby for the children. They come for 1–2 weeks in summer. Primary markets: UK, France, Germany.

**Group of Friends**
6–10 people celebrating a birthday, reunion, or annual trip. They've outgrown resorts and want a private home they can take over entirely. The pool, jacuzzi, sauna, and BBQ are the centre of the experience. Primary markets: UK, France, Germany.

**Couples**
Two people — or two couples — looking for a romantic, peaceful escape. They want beauty, privacy, and something that feels special. The views, the jacuzzi, and the proximity to great restaurants and culture are the main draws. Primary markets: UK, Germany, US.

**Multi-Generational Families**
Grandparents, parents, and grandchildren travelling together. They need a property big enough for everyone, with comfort for older guests and activities for kids. The villa's capacity, outdoor spaces, and easy day trips make it a natural fit. Primary markets: UK, France.

---

## Why People Choose Villa Victoria

**Beach & Quiet Coastal Life**
Roses is a calm, authentic coastal town — not overcrowded or touristy. The beach is close, the sea is clear, and the pace of life is slow. People come here to simply enjoy the sun and the coast in a relaxed setting.

**Privacy & the Freedom of a Home**
Guests who don't want a hotel experience — shared spaces, fixed schedules, strangers around — choose a private villa for the freedom it gives. They cook when they want, eat on the terrace, stay up late, sleep in. The house is theirs.

**Pool, Jacuzzi, Sauna & BBQ**
The private amenities are a core reason to book. A pool no one else uses, a jacuzzi with sea views, a sauna to unwind in, and a BBQ for long evening dinners outside. This is resort-level comfort in a private setting.

**Water Sports**
The Gulf of Roses is one of Spain's best spots for water sports — kayaking, kitesurfing, sailing, snorkelling, diving. Ideal for active families and adventurous groups.

**Hiking & Nature**
The villa sits inside a protected natural park. The Camí de Ronda coastal path, Cap de Creus, and the surrounding trails make it a natural base for walkers and outdoor lovers.

**Sightseeing & Day Trips**
Girona (40 min), Barcelona (1.5 hrs), the Dalí Museum in Figueres, medieval villages — there is no shortage of culture and history to explore. The villa is the perfect base for a trip that mixes relaxation with discovery.

**Sunsets & the View**
The panoramic view over the Gulf of Roses is genuinely extraordinary. For many guests, this alone is a reason to book — and it's what they talk about when they come back.
