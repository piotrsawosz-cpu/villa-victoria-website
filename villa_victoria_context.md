## The Property

**Name:** Villa Victoria
**Address:** Carrer de la Pujada del Puig Rom, 85, 17480 Roses, Girona, Spain
**Region:** Costa Brava, Catalonia, northeastern Spain

Villa Victoria is a beautiful 3-bedroom, 3-bathroom villa perched on the mountain above Roses, offering sweeping panoramic views over the entire Gulf of Roses. It sits within a natural park, which means the air is clean, the surroundings are peaceful, and the area has a distinctly unspoiled quality. Sunsets from the villa are spectacular.

## Location & Surroundings

The villa's location is one of its greatest selling points — it combines seclusion and natural beauty with easy access to a rich array of activities and destinations.

### Nearby Attractions & Activities
- **Beach:** Close by — water sports, boat rentals, swimming
- **Roses town:** Restaurants, shops, markets, historical sites, museums
- **Natural park:** The whole area is a protected natural park — great for hiking, trail running, cycling, and enjoying clean air

### Day Trip Destinations (by car)
- **Girona:** ~40 minutes (medieval city, world-class restaurants)
- **Barcelona:** ~1.5 hours (city breaks, culture, nightlife)
- **Skiing:** ~2 hours (Pyrenees ski resorts)
- **Andorra:** ~4 hours (shopping, skiing, mountain activities)

### Why This Location is Special
Roses is a historically and culturally rich town on the Costa Brava. It is less commercialised than some other Spanish coastal destinations, giving it an authentic, charming character. The area offers the perfect blend of nature, history, gastronomy, and beach life.

## Brand Positioning

**Tone:** Warm, welcoming, and genuine — with a premium feel but never cold or exclusive.

The villa is not positioned as a "luxury" brand in the typical distant, aspirational sense. Instead, the feeling is: *this is a very special, beautiful home that you and your loved ones get to enjoy — a place that feels like family, not a hotel.*

**Brand pillars:**
1. **Beautiful & special** — stunning views, incredible amenities, unforgettable setting
2. **Warm & welcoming** — feels like a home, not a rental

4. **Nature & wellbeing** — natural park, clean air, sauna, jacuzzi, pool
